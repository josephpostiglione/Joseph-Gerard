I'm Joseph

A product designer, composer and filmmaker living in Austin, Texas. I am passionate about researching, testing, and designing accessible and beautiful products.

How I Got Here

After working in the music industry for over ten years I decided it was time for a change. I moved to Austin from NYC and spent a few years working as an electrical technician while trying to find a career that really excited me. Eventually I found the UXUI program at Austin Community College and immediately fell in love with UX design.

Why

My non-traditional work history has given me a diverse and marketable set of skills perfect for the UXUI industry. Working closely with other musicians over the years has tought me empathy and persuasiveness, while my schooling has emphasized the user centered design process and pixel perfect designs. As a lifelong learner with a penchant for problem solving, I am perfectly suited for this ever-evolving field.

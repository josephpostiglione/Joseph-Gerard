About Me

Hiya, I'm Joseph, A UX designer and researcher currently seeking new opportunities in Austin, Texas. I am passionate about researching, testing, and building beautiful and accessible products, systems, and services.

With a background in music performance and production, I was already familiar with the process of storytelling, but it was through my study of User Experience Design that I fell in love with discovering the stories of others. I became fascinated with the way people interact with their surroundings and the emotional journey they have while doing so.

I am comfortable in all phases of the design process, from conducting exploratory research and heuristic analysis to building prototypes and delivering production-ready assets.
